## New database

Connection to new database in user-defined folder.

1. Click "New database";
2. In File Dialog, navigate to desired folder;
3. Hit "OK".

The app then will switch to contact list view.

1. Fill the "Contact properties" in the top part and click "Create". New contact will be created (and saved in database);
2. Fill the "Contact properties" in the top part and click "Update". Selected contact will be updated;
3. Click on a contact will select it;
4. Double click will change all fields to "some ... here".

## Open database

Connection to existing database. Other functionality is the same.

1. Click "New database";
2. In File Dialog, navigate to desired folder and select database;
3. Hit "OK".

The app then will switch to contact list view.

1. Fill the "Contact properties" in the top part and click "Create". New contact will be created (and saved in database);
2. Fill the "Contact properties" in the top part and click "Update". Selected contact will be updated;
3. Click on a contact will select it;
